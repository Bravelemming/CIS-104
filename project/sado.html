A Novel. A setting. A world.

Welcome to Sado! This page introduces a the world of Sado; a fictional setting that will eventually be a book. This is a live updating content site that holds everything that Sado is, was, and will be. Sado is an island, with a people besieged on three fronts by three afflictions. Sado are a people, who survive in a bleak and beautiful environment, where the spirits in things speak to those that listen. Welcome. Explore the world through the links above.
